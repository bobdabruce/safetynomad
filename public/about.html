🔥 SafetyNomad

AI-powered study platform for the University of Fredericton Safety Officer Training Program

Purpose: A personal OHS study tool that turns uploaded course materials — PDFs, DOCX files, legislation, standards — into interactive study sessions. Built for use on any device, at any jobsite, online or offline.

The app covers the full University of Fredericton Safety Officer Training Program:

Internal Responsibility System, worker/employer duties, right to refuse, JHSC, Canadian OHS overview
OHS 105
Organizational Development & Behaviour
Workplace culture, team dynamics, motivation, change management, OHS integration
OHS 110
Leadership and Communications
Safety leadership, communication strategies, worker engagement, behavior-based safety
OHS 115
Incident Investigation & Response
Root cause analysis, investigation methods, ICAM, reporting requirements, corrective actions
OHS 120
Safety Training — Fundamental Principles
OHS foundations, hazard types, workplace inspections, safety culture, training design
OHS 125

SMS frameworks, ISO 45001, OHSAS 18001, PDCA cycle, auditing, continuous improvement
OHS 130

WHMIS 2015, GHS, SDS, TDG, hierarchy of controls, risk matrices, spill response
OHS 135

Canadian OHS legislation, regulatory framework, ethical decision-making, due diligence
OHS 140

OHS 145

Fire behavior, prevention, hazard ID, emergency response, protection systems, Canadian law

Features

📖
AI Study Modules
Generates structured overviews from your uploaded materials with Key Concepts, Canadian scenarios, mnemonics, and a Rapid Review section.
🃏
Flashcards
AI-generated flashcards per topic (20), per course (30), or full program (40). Flip with click, keyboard arrows, or swipe. Difficulty labeled.
❓
Practice Quizzes
Multiple-choice quizzes per topic (12Q), per course (20Q), or full program (30Q). Scored with explanations. Sticky submit bar.
⚡
Rapid Review
Auto-extracted bullet-point summary from the Overview — great for quick pre-exam review.
💬
Course Chat
Ask questions about any course. AI answers using your uploaded materials as context. Stop button. Smart scroll — grab it to pause auto-scroll while reading.
🤖
General AI Assistant
A general-purpose OHS assistant for questions outside specific courses — Canadian law, jobsite scenarios, study advice.
📂
Smart File Import
Upload PDFs, DOCX, TXT, CSV. AI detects which course each file belongs to. Add files one-at-a-time or entire folders. Sources are per-course.
📝
Notes
Personal notes stored per device. Create, edit, tag by course. Supports markdown.
🎓
Full Program Review
Cross-course flashcards and quizzes covering all 10 OHS courses. Tests overall program mastery.
📱
Mobile PWA
Installable on iPhone and Android as a home screen app. Works offline. State is saved to local storage so switching apps doesn't lose your progress.

How to Add Study Materials

  1. Click "＋ Add Files" in the top navigation bar at any time
  2. Pick files (PDF, DOCX, TXT) or an entire folder
  3. AI reads the filename and content, then detects which OHS course each file belongs to
  4. Review the assignments, adjust if needed, then click Upload All
  5. Files are stored on the server and used as context for all AI study generation

You can also add files to a specific course by opening the course and clicking Sources in the top-right.

How Study Content is Generated

When you click "Generate Study Module", "Generate Flashcards", or "Generate Quiz", the app:

  1. Scores all uploaded files for relevance to your topic using keyword matching
  2. Selects the most relevant sources within an 80,000-character context budget
  3. Sends the material to Claude AI with a structured prompt for that content type
  4. Streams the result back in real time (you see it build word by word)
  5. Caches the result locally so you never have to regenerate unless you want to

Legislation & Standards Referenced

The AI is instructed to hyperlink all legislation, standards, and regulatory bodies to their official websites, including:

National Fire Code of Canada Canada Labour Code WorkSafeBC CSA Standards WHMIS 2015 / GHS ISO 45001 OHSAS 18001 TDG Regulations Provincial OHS Acts

Hosted on Railway.app with a persistent volume at /app/data for storing uploaded files, extracted text, course topics, and notes. Survives deploys and restarts.
